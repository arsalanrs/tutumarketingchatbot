COMPANY OVERVIEW:
Morty is an all-in-one mortgage platform designed to power mortgage businesses from day one. The platform is fast, compliant, and scalable, enabling businesses to launch a fully compliant mortgage operation in as little as 48 hours. Morty provides a complete solution including licensing, LOS (Loan Origination System), pricing, digital storefront, automation, and more - all fully integrated and ready to go.

2. Mortgage Infrastructure
